Euro 2020 Preview

Written by EnglandEuro2020 Friday, May 21, 2021

It is almost time for one of the greatest and most historic football tournaments ever played to commence once again, after an unusually long break of 5 years due to the coronavirus pandemic. Nevertheless this summers main footballing attraction promises to deliver the same great entertainment as it always has done, as Europe's greatest sides battle it out to lift the famous European cup!!

The tournament, on its 60th annerversiary, will commence on the 11th June in the Stadio Olimpico in Rome as Turkey and Italy kick us off. The famous tournament will change a lot this year with there being no official host nation and the matches being played out amongst various nations.

Here is everything you need to know ahead of the big day.....

Tournament Dates
The tournament will be played from the 11th June to the 11th July.
Groups
Group A: Turkey, Italy, Wales, Switzerland

Group B: Denmark, Finland, Belgium, Russia

Group C: Netherlands, Ukraine, Austria, North Macedonia

Group D: England, Croatia, Scotland, Czech Republic

Group E: Spain, Sweden, Poland, Slovakia

Group F: Hungary, Portugal, France, Germany

Hosts

The tournament differs this year as it is held all around Europe as opposed to just one country. The selected countries this year are Russia, Azerbaijan, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, Romania, Hungary, Denmark, Scotland, Spain with the semi-finals and final to be played in England.

Official mascot
