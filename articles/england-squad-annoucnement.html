Euro 2020 Ticketing Information

Written by EnglandEuro2020 Friday, May 21, 2021

Due to coronavirus restrictions, Uefa have recalled a large number of tickets originally bought by fans, leaving football supporters everywhere exasperated

Here is Uefa's latest instruction on the situation.....


Fans who are holders of tickets, some who purchased them as far back as 2019 have been officially told they are now cancelled 'as per the ballot process for matches where the number of sold tickets exceeded the new permitted seating capacities'.
Understandably fans are not happy and have taken to social media to vent their anger at seeing these tickets now being sold at extortionate prices of upto Â£1500!!!
This has come as up to 50% of capacity is reduced to fulfill safety procedures in most games and 75% at Wembley. Hopefully with the recent success of the FA cup final hosted at Wembley recently there will be reconsiderations to fan capacity at Euros.


Euro Fan Zones

In response to the boycotting of tickets, fans have revolted and instead of cheering on their team in the stadium, they will now congrugate in designated Euro 2020 Fan Zones around Europe.
Scotland's fan zone located in the heart of glasgow is expected to welcome up to an incredible 180000 fans during the month long competition. Tickets to these events are completely free and can be bought from your local City's Euro 2020 website.
